When does the iPhone Fold come out?

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Answer: September 2026 — at Apple's annual iPhone event, alongside the iPhone 18. Mass production is reported to begin in August 2026. Apple has not confirmed the device or the date; this is the consistent picture from supply-chain reports as of July 2026.

Why September 2026 is credible

Three independent signals line up. First, analyst reports (including Ming-Chi Kuo's supply-chain notes) have held the 2026 window steady for over a year — no slips. Second, display production for a foldable panel of this size reportedly began ramping at Samsung Display specifically for Apple. Third, reporting on the iPhone 18 event describes the foldable as part of the same launch slate, per outlets like MacRumors and PhoneArena.

What could still change

First-generation hardware slips. The two risks reporters flag most: yield on the crease-free display, and hinge durability testing. A slip would more likely mean limited quantities at launch than a different year. We update this page when the picture changes — or get the one-email launch alert.

Waiting vs buying now

Two months from a probable announcement, our advice is simple: if you want Apple, wait. If you want the best foldable today — or the iPhone Fold's rumored $1,999 price is too steep — the Galaxy Z Fold 7, Pixel 10 Pro Fold and Honor Magic V5 are all excellent right now, and Z Fold prices typically dip when Apple enters a category.
