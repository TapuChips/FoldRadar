About FoldRadar

FoldRadar is an independent site tracking foldable phones — with a focus on Apple's upcoming foldable iPhone and the book-style foldables it will compete with. We serve readers in the US, UK and Germany, in English and German.

How we work

Rumors are always labeled as rumors and attributed to their sources (MacRumors, Macworld, PhoneArena, Tom's Guide, analyst supply-chain notes). Estimates — like regional prices before Apple announces them — are marked as our estimates and explained. Pages carry a visible "last updated" date and get corrected as the picture changes.

How we make money

Some product links are affiliate links (including Amazon). A purchase through them can earn us a commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links never influence rankings or verdicts; recommendations are based on published hands-on reviews, durability data and street prices.
