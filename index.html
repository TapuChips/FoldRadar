Why foldables, why now?

Foldables crossed the threshold in 2025–2026: hinges last, creases faded, weight dropped to slab-phone territory, and prices finally started moving. Apple entering the category is the moment foldables go mainstream — analysts expect the first foldable iPhone to reset the category the way the original iPhone reset smartphones.
