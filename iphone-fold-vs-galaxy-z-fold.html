iPhone Fold vs Galaxy Z Fold 7

Last updated: July 20, 2026 · iPhone Fold specs are rumors until Apple's announcement

The honest answer: if you live in the Apple ecosystem, wait the few months — the crease-free display and iOS on a 7.8-inch canvas are worth seeing before you spend $1,999. If you're platform-agnostic and want a foldable now, the Z Fold 7 is proven, excellent, and its price will likely dip when Apple enters the ring.

Where Samsung wins today

It exists. Seven generations of hinge engineering, a 200MP camera with a real telephoto, water resistance, and software that has learned what a folding screen is for. The Z Fold 7 finally matched Samsung's slab flagships on camera quality, which was the last serious compromise. And by September, Z Fold 7 discounts are all but guaranteed.

Where Apple aims to win

The crease — reports say Apple refused to ship one. If true, it removes the most-cited annoyance in every foldable review ever written. Add the ecosystem pull (iMessage, Watch, AirPods), a thinner closed profile, and the App Store's tablet apps on the inner display. The camera, on paper, is the trade-off: no telephoto against Samsung's 3x zoom.

Who should do what

iPhone owners: wait for September — see the timeline. Android users happy to stay: buy the Z Fold 7 or one of our other picks now; don't pay a waiting tax for a phone you won't buy. Undecided with $2,000 ready: the smart play is waiting eight weeks — either you get Apple's foldable, or you get a discounted Z Fold 7 during the response sales.
